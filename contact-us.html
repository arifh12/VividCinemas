FAQ

Who qualifies for a CHILD ticket?
Guests 11 years old and under. Children under 2 are free at Vivid if held by an adult (lap child). If the child requires a separate seat, a CHILD ticket must be purchased.
Who qualifies for a SENIOR ticket?
Guests 55 years+ qualify for a senior ticket.
How old do you have to be to see an R-rated movie?
Guests must be 17+ with valid ID to see an R-rated feature without a parent or guardian. No children 6 years old or younger will be admitted to any R-rated film.
Are there any age restrictions for theatre admittance?
No one 15 years old or younger will be admitted after 9 p.m. unless they are accompanied by a parent or Adult Guardian who agrees to be responsible for the conduct and safety of the underage guest.
Where can I get tickets?
Tickets are available at all box office and kiosks for same location only. Tickets for all locations are also available online.
How can I get a refund or exchange my tickets?
Refunds/Exchanges are available for ticket purchases prior to show start time and with proof of purchase (ticket, receipt, confirmation number). We cannot issue refunds or ticket exchanges via phone, refunds must be issued in person. Ticket purchases made via credit card will be refunded and subject to the return policies of your account. Please note, refunds/exchanges can only be done for the original theatre in which tickets were purchased.
